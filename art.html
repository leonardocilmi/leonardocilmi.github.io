Leonardo Cilmi is a San Francisco based artist working primarily in oils, charcoals, and acrylics. His work focuses on "Tenebristic Impressionism"; impressions of subjects that are illuminated against predominantly dark settings. He has shown his work at several exhibitions and events including Milan Design Week. Contact for pricing, availability, and commissions.
